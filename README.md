# Unexpectedly High Accumulation Rates in the 2022 Mt. Logan Ice Core Reveal Warm-Season Drivers of Precipitation Variability
# Holland et al., 2025 JGR: Atmospheres 
*(In Revision: last updated 12/05/2025)*

This repository contains the supplemental data files associated with 2022 Mt. Logan ice core accumulation record.

Data Citation: Holland, K. M., Criscitiello, A. S., McConnell, J. R., Markle, B. R., Yousif, H. A. K., Skelton, E., Wensman, S. M., Jensen, B. J. L., Winski, D. A., Campbell, S. W., & Chellman, N. J. (2025). 2022 Mt. Logan Ice Core Accumulation Record & Tephra Geochemistry (1912 – 2020 CE) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17835046

## Files Descriptions

## SI_Holland2025_Accumulation.xlsx
Contains the thinning-corrected annually averaged accumulation record of the 2022 Mount Logan ice core covering the period 1912 to 2020 CE.

### Acronym Definitions:
* Hooke: Hooke steady-state ice-flow models from Kaspari, S., Hooke, R. LeB., Mayewski, P. A., Kang, S., Hou, S., & Qin, D. (2008). Snow accumulation rate on Qomolangma 
(Mount Everest), Himalaya: Synchroneity with sites across the Tibetan Plateau on 50–100 year timescales. Journal of Glaciology, 54(185), 343–352. 
https://doi.org/10.3189/002214308784886126
* CFA: Continuous Flow Analysis
* m weq: meters water equivalent

### Parameters:
* Hooke: thinning corrected annual accumulation in m weq
* Uncorrected: annual layer thickness in m weq
* CFA_Top_Depth_mweq: top depth of year in m weq
* CFA_Bottom_Depth_mweq: bottom depth of year in m weq
* CFA_Top_Depth_ice_m: top depth of year in meters ice equivalent
* CFA_Bottom_Depth_ice_m: bottom depth of year in meters ice equivalent

## SI_Holland2025_Tephra.xlsx
Contains the tephra geochemistry dataset associated with the 1912 Novarupta-Katmai and 1986 Augustine tephras.

Please see file for more information.
